# General Coconut Palm Care

## Watering
Young palms need regular watering during the first two years. Mature palms tolerate seasonal drought but yield better with consistent soil moisture. Avoid waterlogging around the root zone.

## Spacing and Maintenance
Plant palms with adequate spacing for airflow and sunlight. Keep the plantation free of weeds. Apply organic mulch away from the trunk base. Inspect crowns and trunks monthly for early signs of disease or pest damage.

## Nutrition
Follow CRI fertilizer recommendations based on palm age and soil tests. Split annual fertilizer into rainy-season applications for best uptake.
