# Bud Rot Disease

## Symptoms
Bud rot affects the crown of coconut palms. Young leaves turn yellow and wilt. The central spear leaf may collapse. A foul smell often comes from the crown. Water stagnation in the crown increases risk.

## Treatment
Remove and destroy infected tissues immediately. Apply Bordeaux mixture (1%) to the affected area. Improve drainage around the tree and avoid water stagnation in the crown. Monitor the palm weekly for progression. Contact your agriculture officer if symptoms spread to neighbouring palms.
