# Stem Bleeding Disease

## Symptoms
Stem bleeding appears as dark reddish-brown liquid oozing from trunk cracks. Bark near the base may soften. In advanced cases the trunk weakens and palm productivity declines. Mechanical injuries and poor drainage increase susceptibility.

## Treatment
Remove affected bark carefully and apply Bordeaux paste to the wound. Improve field drainage and avoid mechanical injuries to the trunk during farm operations. Avoid excessive fertilizer application near the trunk base. Monitor for recurrence over several months.
