# Disease Prevention FAQ

## How can I prevent bud rot?
Keep the crown dry, ensure good drainage, and remove infected material promptly. Avoid overhead irrigation directly into the crown.

## When should I contact an agriculture officer?
Contact an officer when diagnosis is uncertain, disease spreads to multiple palms, or recommended treatments do not improve symptoms within two weeks.

## Can I use chemicals without guidance?
Only use pesticides and fungicides recommended by CRI or your agriculture officer. Incorrect chemical use can harm palms and the environment.

## How do I report a disease outbreak?
Submit photos and symptom details through Coco Care or contact your local agriculture office. Verified reports help protect neighbouring farms.
