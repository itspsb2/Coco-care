# Coconut Caterpillar Damage

## Symptoms
Coconut caterpillar damage shows as irregular holes and brown dried leaves. Fronds may look shredded. Severe infestations reduce photosynthesis and nut yield. Dry seasons often see higher caterpillar activity.

## Treatment
Remove and burn severely affected fronds away from the plantation. Use recommended biological control agents when available. Monitor palms regularly during dry seasons when infestations peak. Maintain field hygiene by clearing fallen fronds.
