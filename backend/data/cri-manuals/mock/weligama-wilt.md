# Weligama Coconut Leaf Wilt Disease

## Symptoms
Weligama Coconut Leaf Wilt Disease causes yellowing and wilting of mature leaves. Leaf tips dry and hang downward. Nuts may drop prematurely. The disease spreads slowly through plantations in the southern and western regions.

## Treatment
Remove affected fronds and destroy them away from the plantation. Do not use removed material as mulch. Apply recommended fungicide per CRI guidelines. Control leaf-feeding insects that may spread the disease. Report suspected cases to the Coconut Research Institute or local agriculture officer.
