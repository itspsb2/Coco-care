### Distance to Apply Fertilizer for Coconut Seedlings

| Age of Tree | Distance from the base (cm) |
| :--- | :--- |
| 6 Months | 30 |
| 1 Year | 60 |
| 2 Years | 90 |
| 3 Years | 120 |
| 4 Years until Flowering | 150 |
| Bearing Tree | 180 |

### Application Method: For Seedling Trees
* Spread the recommended amount of fertilizer within the recommended radius around the tree, based on its age.
*(Example: For a 1-year-old tree, spread the recommended amount of fertilizer within a 2 ft (0.6m) radius from the base of the tree.)* By the time the tree grows and reaches the flowering stage, this distance will be 5 ft (1.5m) from the base.
* Turn the soil up to a depth of 4-6 inches so that the fertilizer and soil mix well.
* The fertilized area should be mulched using slashed weeds, dry coconut fronds, or coconut husks.
