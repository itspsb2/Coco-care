# How to Identify Coconut Varieties (Cultivars)? Can Any Variety Be Planted Anywhere?

Cultivars should be selected based on the agro-ecological zone where the land is located, soil factors, and the size of the land. For each cultivar, the quality of every seedling is certified with a specifically colored sticker.

**1. CRIC 60 (Tall Coconut)**
* These tall cultivars are recommended for all coconut-growing areas in Sri Lanka.

**2. CRISL 98 (Moorock Tall)**
* These tall cultivars are recommended for large-scale cultivations in the Wet and Intermediate zones of Sri Lanka.

**3. CRIC 65 (Kapruwana) / KAPSETHA / CRISL 2020**
* These short, hybrid cultivars are recommended for home gardens in any area, and for large-scale cultivation in the Wet and Intermediate zones. They can also be recommended for other areas in Sri Lanka under high crop management conditions with a permanent water supply.
* *Note: Since planting seed coconuts of hybrid cultivars will not yield hybrid palms, it is strictly NOT recommended to use these cultivars for obtaining seed coconuts.*
