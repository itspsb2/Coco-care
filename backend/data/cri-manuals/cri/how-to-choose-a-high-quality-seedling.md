# How to Choose a High-Quality Seedling?

**Characteristics of a High-Quality Coconut Seedling:**
* Having a thick, strong stem
* Having dark green, broad leaves
* Sharp orientation of the leaf split
* Convex shape of the leaves
* Having short leaf stalks (petioles) connecting the fronds to the stem
* Being free from pests and diseases

**Where to Obtain Coconut Seedlings:**
To get informed about obtaining seedlings from a nursery near you, contact:
