# How to Manage Weeds in Coconut Cultivation?

Weed management should be done considering the life cycles of weeds, their spread in the field, and environmental factors.
* **Mechanical methods:** Brush cutters, disc ploughing, harrowing, and tractor slashing are commonly used.
* **Other methods:** Systematically growing cover crops (Pueraria, Calopogonium, Centrosema) and intercrops, grazing by animals, and moisture conservation methods like mulching also manage weeds effectively.
