# Weligama Coconut Leaf Wilt Disease

### Details
Reported in Weligama in 2006, this is caused by a Phytoplasma pathogen and has spread to most divisions in the Southern Province.

##### Symptoms
 Leaflets become flat and bend like ribs; lower fronds turn yellow (rarely middle fronds too); margins of yellowed leaflets dry up.

##### Control
 Identify infected trees, remove them from the field, and burn the crown to prevent spreading to other areas.
